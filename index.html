Definitions

Fuel-rule Stand-by equipment must store enough energy for ≥ 12 h full-load autonomy (Uptime Tier Standards §4.2.2).
QC-gate Internal validation rule: stand-by MW may not exceed 1.5 × installed IT MW to avoid double-counting.
EU-TRL European Technology Readiness Level scale (1 = basic idea, 9 = mass deployment).
CAPEX basis Full EPC cost including fuel system, exhaust treatment, fire protection; real-2024 €.
OPEX basis Fuel + consumables at 700 h stand-by runtime per year; excludes staff.
Lifecycle CO₂ Cradle-to-grave emissions (ISO 14044) per delivered kWh back-up.
Footprint Surface required for a reference 1 MW block, including fuel or battery storage.
Grid-service income Documented average earnings from frequency-response or reserve markets where technology qualifies (UK DS3 2023, PJM RegD 2024).
